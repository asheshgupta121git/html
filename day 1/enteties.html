html entities

  • An html Entity is a piece of text("string") that begins with an ampersand(&) And ends with A semicolon(;).
  • Used to Display Reverse character (Which would otherwise be interpreted as html code). and invisible characters (like non-breaking spaces.)
  • Can also use in Place of characters that are difficult to type with a standard keyboard
  • Browser interprets and render correct character.
